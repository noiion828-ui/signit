## Detailed Description (max. 16,000 characters)

```
SignIt — Your Voice. Your Data. No more excuses.

You want to sign a petition — but the form is tedious. Type your name, email, postal code, city, street. Then hunt for the confirmation email. Every single time. For every petition.

SignIt does it in seconds.


WHAT SIGNIT DOES

One-click autofill
Visit any petition on openPetition.de. SignIt detects the form automatically and fills it in — first + last name, email, postal code, city. You just click "Sign".

Multi-step forms? Handled.
openPetition uses a multi-step AJAX form: Step 1 (name + email), Step 2 (address, injected after reCAPTCHA). SignIt detects both steps automatically via MutationObserver and fills in Step 2 as soon as it appears.

Gmail / Outlook deeplink
After signing, you need the confirmation email from openPetition. SignIt shows a toast with a direct link to your inbox — Gmail or Outlook — so you never lose your vote.

Impact tracking
SignIt remembers which petitions you signed and checks the current signature counts every 4 hours. In the popup you see: how many new signatures have been collected since you signed. You know your voice made a difference.

Live badge
The number of new signatures appears as a green badge on the extension icon — even when the popup is closed.


HOW IT WORKS

1. Install the extension
2. Open the popup → set up your profile once (name, email, postal code, city, street optional)
3. Visit any petition on openpetition.de
4. SignIt autofills the form automatically
5. Sign the petition, confirm via email
6. Open the Petitions tab in the popup: your impact counter is running


PRIVACY — 100% LOCAL

All your data (name, email, address) is stored exclusively locally in chrome.storage.local. No server. No database. No telemetry. No account required.

SignIt only reads petition pages to count signatures (direct fetch of the public page). No personal data is transmitted.

Permissions:
- storage: local storage of your profile and petition list
- activeTab: access to the active petition page for autofill
- alarms: periodic impact polling (every 4 hours)
- Host permission for openpetition.de: autofill and signature counting

No additional permissions. No "all sites" access.


COMPATIBILITY

- openPetition.de (fully supported: Step 1 + Step 2 + Confirmation Toast)
- More petition platforms coming in future versions


WHO IS SIGNIT FOR?

For everyone who regularly signs petitions and doesn't want to waste time on repetitive forms. Activists, engaged citizens, curious individuals — anyone who wants to sign quickly and reliably.


OPEN SOURCE

SignIt is open source. Source code, issues, and roadmap on GitHub:
https://github.com/noiion/signit


CONTACT & SUPPORT

Website: https://signit.tools
Problems or feedback? Issues on GitHub or via signit.tools.
```
